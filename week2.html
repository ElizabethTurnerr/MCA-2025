Week 2 – Music Notation

Task - 1

This week, For the second week of Labs, I transcribed 10 bars of "Moon River" into MuseScore. I came across challenges such as: aligning rhythms and dotted values. Tools: MuseScore (.mscz) uploaded to /data/MoonRiver.mscz and MusicXML /data/MoonRiver.mxl..

Editing the Score

I imported the music and edited 10 bars to correct rhythm and pitch. This task helped me better understand how symbolic data represents melody, harmony, dynamics in structured form.

Reflections

Admitingly, downloading MuseScore in the first place proved difficulty. However, once that was up and running, MuseScore was easy to use once I understood the basic tools, especially for rhythmic editing. A lot of this required manual adjustment. Overall, this exercise taught me how symbolic music formats like MusicXML and MEI are constructed.
